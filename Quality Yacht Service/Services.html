Exterior Wash

$150 – $500
Duration: 1–6 hours (depending on yacht size)

A thorough exterior wash to remove salt, dirt, and grime from your yacht's surfaces. Perfect for regular maintenance between detailed services.

Service Includes:

  • Complete hull and superstructure wash
  • Marine-safe detergent application
  • Light stainless steel polish
  • Window cleaning (exterior)
  • Deck and cockpit surface cleaning
  • Light debris removal from drains and scuppers

Exterior Detailing

$400 – $1,000
Duration: 3–8 hours (depending on yacht size and condition)

Premium exterior detailing service to restore your yacht's showroom shine. Includes comprehensive cleaning, polishing, and protection treatments.

Service Includes:

  • Deep cleaning of all exterior surfaces
  • Stainless steel polishing and protection
  • Multi-stage compounding and polishing (3-step process)
  • Ceramic coating or wax application
  • Anchor and chain cleaning
  • Complete teak refinishing (sanding, oiling, or caulking)
  • Rub rail and hardware detailing
  • Final sealant application for UV protection
Note: This is our most comprehensive exterior service. Recommended quarterly or before special events/charters. Price varies based on yacht size and condition.

Interior Cleaning

$200 – $600
Duration: 2–4 hours

Keep your yacht's interior fresh and sanitary with our thorough deep cleaning service. We pay attention to every detail to ensure a pristine living environment on board.

Service Includes:

  • Cabin vacuuming
  • Head (bathroom) deep clean
  • Galley degreasing & appliance wipe-down
  • All surface dusting and polishing
  • Interior window and mirror cleaning
  • Bed linen service (optional: change and laundry service)
Optional Add-on: Upholstery treatment available for an additional $75–$150 depending on surface area.

Hull Cleaning & Inspection (Dive Service)

$250 – $700
Duration: 1–3 hours

Maintain optimal performance and fuel efficiency with regular hull cleaning. Our certified divers provide thorough underwater cleaning and inspection services.

Service Includes:

  • Underwater hull scrub
  • Propeller cleaning
  • Visual inspection for damage/barnacles
  • Light growth removal
  • Through-hull fitting inspection
  • Rudder and keel inspection
  • Zinc anode check
Note: Certified diver using environmentally safe methods. Additional charges may apply for heavy growth or extensive barnacle removal.
