Meet Our Founder

Meet Tray Curry

Founder & Lead Technician

Tray Curry, a lifelong boating enthusiast and Abaco native, founded Quality Yacht Services to fill a gap in the local market: reliable, mobile, and professional yacht care.

Growing up around the waters of the Bahamas, Tray developed a deep appreciation for marine vessels and their maintenance needs. After years working in marine services and recognizing the challenges yacht owners faced finding consistent, high-quality care, he decided to create a service that puts the client first.

With years of hands-on experience and a commitment to excellence, Tray ensures every vessel receives the attention it deserves. He personally oversees many of the services and maintains direct relationships with clients, ensuring consistent quality and accountability.

"Your yacht deserves the same care and attention you give it. We're not just cleaning boats—we're preserving investments and ensuring memorable experiences on the water."

Our Mission

To provide professional, reliable, and high-quality yacht maintenance and detailing services, ensuring clients' vessels remain pristine, protected, and performing well.

We believe that exceptional yacht care shouldn't be complicated or inconvenient. That's why we bring our expertise directly to your dock, using premium products and proven techniques to maintain and enhance your vessel's value and appearance.

Whether you're a private owner, charter company, or marina manager, we're committed to being your trusted partner for all yacht maintenance needs in the Bahamas.
