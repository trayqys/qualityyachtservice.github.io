Booking FAQs

Have questions about booking your yacht service? Find answers to common questions below.

How soon can you service my yacht?

We typically schedule appointments within 2-5 business days, depending on service type and availability. For urgent requests, call us directly at (242) 555-YACHT. Monthly plan clients receive priority scheduling.

Do I need to be present during the service?

No, you don't need to be present. We work with marinas to access your yacht. We recommend providing access instructions in the special requests section of your booking. All our technicians are licensed, insured, and background-checked for your peace of mind.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on yacht size, service type, and current condition. The ranges shown are estimates for yachts 30-60ft. We'll provide an exact quote after reviewing your yacht details. There are no hidden fees—the price we quote is the price you pay.

What happens after I submit my booking request?

1. You'll receive an email confirmation of your request
2. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm details
3. We'll provide an exact quote based on your yacht specifics
4. Once confirmed, you'll receive a calendar invitation and service reminder
5. Our team arrives at your dock and completes the service

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without penalty. For cancellations within 24 hours, a $50 fee may apply. We understand emergencies happen—just contact us as soon as possible. Monthly plan clients enjoy flexible rescheduling.
